The Life and Career of Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham, who goes by the stage name of Drake, has been in the spotlight since he was a young boy. He starred in the hit show Degrassi at 15 years old. At 19, he began rapping and singing, which was just the start to his illustrious career. Drake is known for his musical versatility, emotional and relatable lyrics, and his overall consistent commercial success.

Early Life And Career

Drake was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada. Drake was exposed to music and acting at a young age, which led him to the career he now has. In 2001, Drake starred in the hit TV show, Degrassi. Drake gained some fame from this show, but his career really took off in 2006 when he entered the music world.

Drake’s Hit Career

After releasing a handful of mixtapes, Drake hit it off when he came out with the 2009 mixtape So Far Gone, featuring some of his most well-known tracks, including Best I Ever Had. From there he signed with Lil Wayne’s Young Money Entertainment, where Drake took his career to a whole new level. Now in 2025, Drake has over 300 records in the Billboard Hot 100, the most ever by any artist. He also has the most #1 singles on the Billboard Hot 100. In addition to his music career, Drake has created his own clothing brand, OVO (October’s Very Own), OVO sound (his own record label), he is a Global Ambassador for the Toronto Raptors, all while dominating the music industry, making him one of the greatest and most influential artists of all time.
