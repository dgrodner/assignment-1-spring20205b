The Life and Career of Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham, who goes by the stage name of Drake, has been in the spotlight since he was a young boy. He starred in the hit show Degrassi at 15 years old. At 19, he began rapping and singing, which was just the start of his illustrious career. Drake is known for his musical versatility, emotional and relatable lyrics, and his overall consistent commercial success.

Early Life and Career

Drake was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto, Canada. Drake was exposed to music and acting at a young age, which led him to the career he now has. In 2001, Drake starred in the hit TV show, Degrassi. Drake gained some fame from this show, but his career really took off in 2006 when he entered the music world.

Drake’s Hit Career

After releasing a handful of mixtapes, Drake hit it off when he came out with the 2009 mixtape So Far Gone, featuring some of his most well-known tracks, including Best I Ever Had. From there, he signed with Lil Wayne’s Young Money Entertainment, where Drake took his career to a whole new level. Now in 2025, Drake has over 300 records in the Billboard Hot 100, the most ever by any artist.
